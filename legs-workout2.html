Lunges

Steps:

1. Keep your upper body straight and with your shoulders back and relaxed

2. Step forward with one leg

3. Lowering your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle

4. Make sure your front knee is directly above your ankle, not pushed out too far

5.Make sure your other knee doesn't touch the floor.

6. Keep the weight in your heels as you push back up to the starting position.
