Gluteal Kickbacks

- Steps:

  1. Kneeling on a mat or towel with your palms face
    down on the floor directly under your shoulders

  2. Keeping your knee at 90 degress slowly raise
    your foot into the air as high as it will go

  3. Slowly lower it back to the starting position

  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you can no longer
    maintain correct technique

  5. Now swap legs
