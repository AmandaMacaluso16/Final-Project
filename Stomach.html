Get Those Washboard Abs!

1). Crunches
Although it may seem simple, crunches are
the best solution for loosing unwanted stomac fat.
Learning how to do them properly is what will lead
to your ultimate success. Using weights while performing
the crunches is also ideal, but they can produce successful results even without weights.
* An exercise ball is optional*

2). Planks
Planks are the best solution for obtaining those washboard abs.
Planks are a difficult exercise and require a buildup of stamina in
order to best optimize results. This exercise only recomends the
use of a yoga mat so one's feet and hands do not slide.
