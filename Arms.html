Tone Those Arms

Each of these basic exercises has multiple variations that, put together, target all of the arm and shoulder muscles for speedy results.
One should do these routines 4 times a week on nonconsecutive days.

1. Arm Curl
Arms curls focus on tonings one's biceps and triceps.
Arms curls not only tone but shed unwanted pounds
on one's arms. * This exercise requires the use of weights*

2. Shoulder Shaper
Shoulder shapes focus on toning the shoulder. With a combination
of the shoulder and the arm curl the entire arm will be sculpted
to perfection. *This exercise requires the use of weights*
